## 2. IDEA VAULT

### 2.1 Default View
*   A grid of all ideas.
*   **Status Colors:** Yellow (Incubating), Green (Ready), Grey (Completed).

### 2.2 Select Mode
*   Allows **Bulk Actions**.
*   Select multiple ideas -> Click "Move to Ready" to push them all to the Studio at once.

---

## 3. HORIZON (Calendar)

### 3.1 Visual Coding
*   **Center Dot:** Platform (Blue = LinkedIn, Red = YouTube, etc.)
*   **Outer Ring:** Content Pillar Strategy (Does the ring color match your strategy?)
*   **Small Dot:** Action Notes (Something needs attention).

### 3.2 Loading Dock
*   The list on the left side of the calendar.
*   Contains posts that are **Drafted** but **Unscheduled**.
*   **Modes:**
    *   *Backlog:* Standard Drafts.
    *   *Resurface:* Evergreen posts ready for recycling.
*   **Action:** Drag from Dock -> Calendar.

---

## 4. SYSTEM HEALTH

### 4.1 Pillar Alignment Chart
*   **Grey Bars:** Your Goal (Strategy).
*   **Colored Bars:** Your Actuals (Reality).
*   **Goal:** Keep the colored bars close to the grey bars.

### 4.2 Velocity Map
*   A "Scatter Plot" showing when you posted.
*   **Dots:** colored by platform.
*   **Use Case:** Spot gaps in consistency.

### 4.3 Production Audit (Pillar Freshness)
*   **Green Badge (Fresh):** Last post < 14 days ago.
*   **Yellow Badge (Stale):** Last post 14-30 days ago.
*   **Red Badge (Neglected):** No posts in > 30 days.

### 4.4 Incubation & Schedule Audits
*   **Incubation Audit:** Shows a list of ideas that are "Stuck" (oldest first).
*   **Schedule Audit:** Shows a list of posts that are "Overdue" (missed deadlines).
